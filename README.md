# Shaghayegh Mohammadi
Geneticist & Bioinformatician, Integrating Clinical Epigenetics, Bioinformatics, and Molecular Biology focusing on neurodegenerative disorders 

## About Me
I am a passionate researcher focused on the genetic and epigenetic mechanisms underlying neurodegenerative disorders such as Multiple Sclerosis, Alzheimer's, and Parkinson's disease. My expertise lies in combining molecular laboratory techniques with advanced computational tools to analyze complex biological data, aiming to improve preclinical detection and elucidate disease pathophysiology.

Currently, I am exploring the intersection of science and education, with experience in mentoring and teaching, aspiring to bridge the gap between research and effective knowledge transfer.

## Skills & Expertise

- **Molecular Techniques**: qPCR, RT-PCR, PCR, Blood DNA/RNA Extraction, cDNA Synthesis, Primer Design
- **Bioinformatics & Data Science**: R (Advanced), Python (Beginner), NGS Data Analysis (Beginner), Statistical Modeling, Meta-Analysis, Data Visualization (ggplot2, heatmap), ROC Curve Analysis
- **Software & Tools:** Oligo7, SnapGene, MEGA X, Office Packages (Professional)
- **Teaching & Communication:** Science Communication, Public Speaking, Lesson Planning, Team Leadership
